Academic Pojects

Inertia Control Of Hybrid AC/DC Microgrid

Under Guidance of Dr. T. Rajeev 2022

Designed and tested a new control algorithm for inertia control in hybrid microgrids using supercapacitors. The developed algorithm was implemented on a TI Delfino class microcontroller using simulink. The controller was tested using Hardware-in-Loop testing on realtime simulation platform OPAL-RT.

Full Spectrum Simulator for Hydroelectric Power Plant

Guided by Dr. Bijuna Kunju 2018

We developed a transient model of a power plant, and designed a software capable of performing Control Hardware in Loop testing on modern-day Integrated Electronic Devices (relays and measurements units) and control room Human Machine Interfaces. This project was funded and supported by Centre for Engineering Research and Development, Government of Kerala.

My Hobby Projects

Electrification of Community Hall, Rosemala, Kerala, India May 2018

We electrified a community hall at Rosemala, Kerala, India as part of soial outrach project of IEEE Student Branch of our College. This is a remote settlement with the nearest town away at a drive of 3 hours, including an hour of offroad steap hilly roads. I was in-charge of Installation Design, Testing and Commissioning.

Hanging Wall plotter Mar 2017

Inspired from POLARGRAPH, we designed a wall plotter - Vertical CNC machine-, the same was developed around Arduino MEGA and RAMPS 1.4, the commonly used platforms for DIY CNC machines.

DTMF Controlled Home Automation Unit Dec 2016

We improved our Home Automation Unit we designed earlier to communicate over Telephone Lines and to be controlled from literally anywhere. The deice was upgraded to include DTMF codes as input. We included a SIM800 Module and used its internal decoder so as to keep the module standalone and compact. A tutorial on the same have posed on instructables

DTMF Controlled Robo Car Nov 2016

We designed a DTMF controlled Robo Car, This was designed around Arduino and using DTMF decoder Module. The car chassis was home made, as part of another project. This was also the on project testing of our Robo chassis.

Design of Robo Car Chassis Aug 2016

Car Chassis is always a topic of interest for youngsters, this was the very reason why we tried our hands at designing our own Chassis for our Robo-Car applications. This exercise was fun as we had to put our effort in getting almost all chassis designs used in DIY kits and made a design which we felt had all pros of designs we came across.

C program for Load Flow Analysis using Gauss-Seidel Iterations May 2016

We developed a C program that can do Load Flow Analysis for a predefined system employing Gauss-Seidel Iterations algorithm.
